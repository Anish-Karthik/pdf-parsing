Your spine is a complex and amazing structure, made up of a stack of bones called vertebrae. These vertebrae are grouped into different regions, each with its own unique role.

The lower back is supported by the lumbar vertebrae, and there are five of these strong bones. They carry a lot of weight and allow for a wide range of movement.

Understanding the different parts of the spine, like the number of lumbar vertebrae, can help you appreciate the incredible design of your body.
