Our bodies are amazing movement machines, thanks to skeletal muscle. These powerful muscles are responsible for everything from walking to lifting weights.

Here's what makes skeletal muscle special:

  • Striped: Under a microscope, skeletal muscle looks like it has stripes.
  • Voluntary: You control skeletal muscles - you decide when to move them!
  • Locomotion: Skeletal muscle is the star of movement, letting you walk, run, and jump.
  • Posture: It also helps keep you upright and balanced.

So, the answer to the question is skeletal muscle. Think of the biceps in your arm - that's a great example of a skeletal muscle that helps you flex your arm.
