Your face is a marvel of engineering, and the mandible, or lower jaw, is one of its most important parts. It's the only bone in your skull that can move, allowing you to chew, talk, and even smile!

The mandible is strong and sturdy, holding your lower teeth and connecting to your skull at the temporomandibular joint (TMJ). This allows for a wide range of movement, making it essential for eating, speaking, and expressing yourself.
